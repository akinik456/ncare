# Current Limitations

Requests are currently written to a **global requests collection**.

Future improvement will move to a **requester-based structure**.

Example planned structure:
requesters/{requesterId}/requests/{requestId} requesters/{requesterId}/responses/{requestId} requesters/{requesterId}/locators/{locatorId}


This will allow:

• cleaner architecture  
• multiple requester devices  
• no cross-device interference
